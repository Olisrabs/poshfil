how it works

POSHFIL transforms banana peels into a natural shoe polish by extracting their nutrients, blending them with organic oils, and creating a safe, eco-friendly formula that shines and protects leather.

Nature’s Secret Ingredient

We collect and upcycle banana peels – a common waste – transforming them into a nutrient-rich, natural base for our polish.

Crafted with Care

Through an innovative, eco-friendly process, we blend the banana peel extract with organic oils to create a smooth, effective polish.

Shine, Nourish, Protect

Simply apply POSHFIL to your shoes for a deep, chemical-free shine that restores leather, protects against wear, and keeps your style spotless.
